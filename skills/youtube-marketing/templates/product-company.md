# Channel Template — Product Company

## When to Use
Use this template when:
- The channel promotes a plugin, theme, SaaS, or software product
- Revenue goal = product sales / free installs, NOT ad CPM
- Audience = developers, designers, or technical buyers

## Channel Constants (Customize Per Channel)

```
Channel name: [Your channel name]
Products: [Product 1], [Product 2], [Product 3]
Pricing pages: [URL per product]
Docs: [URL per product]
Community: [Facebook group / Discord URL]
Support: [Helpdesk URL]
Discount code: [CODE] ([N]% off)
Subscribe URL: https://www.youtube.com/c/[handle]/?sub_confirmation=1
```

## Fixed Description Links (Use in Every Video)

```
▶ [Product 1 name]: [pricing URL]
▶ [Product 2 name]: [URL]
▶ Docs & Help: [docs URL]
▶ Community: [community URL]
▶ Feature Roadmap: [roadmap URL]
▶ Premium Support: [helpdesk URL]

🎁 USE CODE '[DISCOUNT_CODE]' FOR AN EXTRA [N]% DISCOUNT
```

## Content Ratio
- 2 broad how-to tutorials (high search volume, not product-specific)
- 1 widget/feature demo (product-specific)
- 1 Short per long-form video

## CTA Strategy
- Primary CTA: Product pricing page (free download or trial)
- Secondary CTA: Subscribe
- CTA placement: 25% mark in video + end screen
- Never hard sell — show the result, let the demo do the selling

## Revenue Priority
1. Direct product sales (highest LTV)
2. Email list capture (lead magnet → purchase sequence)
3. Affiliate (hosting, complementary tools — no product conflict)
4. Ad revenue (passive, not the goal)
